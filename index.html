How to apply LLMs in Education domain?

Intruduction

As well as we know, LLM technique developed rapidly and change our work style in unimageable ways. Like ChatGPT, Copliot, more and more LLMs play their roles in many fileds, here I thought that LLM could promote the education development by its powerful and accurate QA ability.

Details

In about last 4 years in Covid-SARs time, most of the students had to attend class online, also in this time, most students found that the feedback in time and detail is helpful for their studies. However, the number and the eneries of teachers is limited, student could not get the whole detailed information about their question.

So I think that maybe the LLMs could help to relieve this problem, desiging a LLM which act as like ChatTeacher, train data with real and massive QA data from teacher and students. One challenging point is that we hope LLM could not give the students directly, they should like the teacher introduce students to think and work out the homework by themselves. It sounds like that for a question LLMs give users prompts rather than users give LLMs.

Conclusion

I think many students need this ChatTeacher since it could be a powerful tool in their studying. The LLM is not only useful but also full of treasureful. I bet a lot of students would pay for thist LLM.
